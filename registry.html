Registry

Caspian is a member of the following registries:

  • AKC Foundation Stock
  • UKC
  • CKC

Pedigree information for Caspian is available at Pawpeds. Just type "Caspian" in the search box. Pawpeds has an international database for Picards. To date there are over 2036 dogs registered. Every dog listed has its full pedigree on file as well as number of offspring that have been registered. It is one of the wonderful tools used by breeders when deciding on a potential mate.

Cassie has his AKC Good Citizen Certificate (CGC) - Now an AKC title. Cassie also passed his therapy dog certification with Therapy Dog International (TDI) and is involved with therapy work, sheep herding, advanced obedience, agility, and will hopefully be learning all about K-9 Nose Work soon!
